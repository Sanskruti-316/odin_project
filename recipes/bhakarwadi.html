Bhakarwadi Recipe

About Bhakarwadi

    
Call it Bhakarwadi or Bakarwadi, the fact that this snack is a super popular one in western India, doesn’t change. 
From the looks of it, it basically is a crisp, fried pinwheel or spiral and may really appear to involve a difficult procedure
to prepare it. This, may not be completely false. But if you are ready to put in some efforts and follow this Bhakarwadi Recipe to bits,
you’ll be able to recreate the magic of this addictive, sweet, spicy and tangy snack at your home.
  • Ingredients for Bhakarwadi:
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
    • 1/4 cup gram flour (besan)
    • 1/4 cup semolina (rava)
    • 2 tbsp oil
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
    • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
    • Salt to taste
    • Water as needed
    • Oil for deep frying
    • For the filling:
      • 1 cup grated coconut
      • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts (ground)
      • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
      • 1 tbsp jaggery (grated)
      • 1 tsp garam masala
      • 1 tsp cumin seeds
      • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
      • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
      • Salt to taste
  • Instructions to make Bhakarwadi:
    • In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, gram flour, semolina, turmeric powder, red chili powder, salt, and oil. Add water gradually to form a stiff dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
    • For the filling, mix grated coconut, ground peanuts, sesame seeds, jaggery, garam masala, cumin seeds, fennel seeds, red chili powder, and salt in a bowl.
    • Divide the dough into equal portions. Roll out each portion into a thin rectangle.
    • Spread a portion of the filling evenly over the rolled-out dough.
    • Roll the dough tightly into a log and seal the edges with a little water.
    • Cut the log into thin slices to form pinwheels.
    • Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Fry the bhakarwadis in batches until golden brown and crispy.
    • Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
    • Let them cool completely before storing in an airtight container.

    Enjoy your delicious homemade Bhakarwadi!
